ENJOY BOOKS FOR FREE

Here you will find a wide range of books to suit your taste, as well as a lot of scientific literature that can be borrowed free of charge from our library.

About Our Library
Welcome to our library, where books become accessible to all, regardless of financial means. Borrow books for free for a period ranging from 1 to 4 weeks.

The maximum number of books you can have at one time is 10. The maximum number of books you can keep at a time varies from 1 to 10 hours depending on the history of successful and unsuccessful returns.

Our goal is to make reading accessible to everyone, so join us, borrow books, and immerse yourself in the world of words with our library.

Rules and Incentives
Learn about our rules and incentives for good return practices. If you have 5 overdue returns, your loan duration decreases from 4 weeks to 3. With 10 overdue returns, it decreases to 2 weeks, and with 20 overdue returns, it decreases to 1 week.

If you have 5 overdue returns, the maximum number of simultaneous loans decreases to 4 books. With 10 overdue returns, it decreases to 3 books, and with 20 overdue returns, it decreases to 1 book.

On the positive side, for every 5 successful returns, your maximum simultaneous loans increase. With 5 successful returns, it increases to 6 books; with 10, it increases to 8 books, and with 20, it increases to 10 books.
